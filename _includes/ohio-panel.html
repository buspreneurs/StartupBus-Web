Panel Discussion

Thanks to Boxcast the Akron bus will be live streaming a panel discussion (right here!) on the maker and IoT movement at 5pm EST May 14th. The panel is geared toward helping riders of the MakerBus prepare for the epic journey they have ahead of them and to help them come up with awesome ideas for projects. We'll also be discussing entrepreneurship and how to take something from just a project done on a bus to a fully functioning business (like our friends at Wastebits).

Panelists:

Ken Burns

Founded Tiny Circuts in 2011. Tiny Circuts is an Open Source Hardware company specializing in designing and manufacturing very small electronics. Based out of Akron, OH.

Chris Bue

Runs MakerBiz Chicago which is a support network for of an for entrepreneurs. She's also avidly involved in StartupBus. She conducted the 2015 MakerBus and will be acting as a mentor on this year's MakerBus.

Gaurav Saxena
(aka G)

Founded the Akron Makerspace. The Akron Makerspace is a nonprofit makerspace in the heart of downtown Akron dedicated to being a place where people can educate, create, and share amongst themselves and others within the domains of technology, art and science.

Andrea Lane

Andrea runs a mobile fab lab which she drives to schools to teach kids about how to bring their ideas to life.
